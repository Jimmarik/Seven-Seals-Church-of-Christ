Ministry Overview

The Seven Seals Church of christ and Reconciliation is a Christian sanctuary founded back on 14th April 2002 By Bishop Wilson Sowa through the guidelines of the Holy Spirit. The Church is headquartered in Pipeline Embakasi, Nairobi Kenya with Various branches a cross the country and across the borders in Congo and Burundi. The Church is built on the foundation of the word of God as the guidelines to the lifestyles and spiritual well-being of our congregants. (Psalms 119:105 "Your word is a lamp to my feet And a light to my path.") We believe if the working of God through his Holy Spirit to bring all the spiritual gifts granted by the Holy Spirit of God. The Seven Seals Church is based on the book of Revelation Chapter 5. Where by a scroll written on both sides had been sealed with the Seven Seals. An arch angel proclaimed in a loud voice “Who is worthy to open the scroll and to loose its seals?” No one on earth or heaven was found worthy. (... in the midst of the throne and of the four living creatures, and in the midst of the elders, stood a Lamb as though it had been slain, having seven horns and seven eyes, which are the seven Spirits of God sent out into all the earth. 7 Then He came and took the scroll out of the right hand of Him who sat on the throne).

  • Mission:

    • To Preach the gospel to all the people of the nations, maintaining the standards of Christ through teaching and practicing Holiness as outlined in the word of God.

  • VISION:

    • (Mathew 28:18-20 18 And Jesus came and spoke to them, saying, “All authority has been given to Me in heaven and on earth. 19 Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, 20 teaching them to observe all things that I have commanded you; and lo, I am with you always, even to the end of the age.” Amen.)

  • STATEMENT OF FAITH:

    • Believe in God the father, the creator of heaven, earth and everything that is in Existence through Christ our Lord and Savior. (1st Peter 1:21 ... who through Him believe in God, who raised Him from the dead and gave Him glory, so that your faith and hope are in God.)

  • OBJECTIVES:

    • Bring Reconciliation of human beings and God through repentance of sin. Spread the Word of God to all who are yet to receive the true Gospel of salvation Raise a team of ministers including apostles, some prophets, some evangelists, and some pastors and teachers to minister the gospel. To Raise a God fearing generation of youths through teaching from childhood level. Heal broken marriages and become a solution to mental and psychosocial issues in the church and community.
